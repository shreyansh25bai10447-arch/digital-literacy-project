# Social Media Do's and Don'ts
## For College Students | CSE0001 Digital Literacy
### VIT Bhopal University | [Your Name] | [Reg No.]

---

## ✅ DO'S

1. **Keep your profiles professional and updated**
   Recruiters and professors often check your social 
   media before internships or opportunities.

2. **Think before you post**
   Ask yourself "would my professor or parent 
   see this?" Once posted, content is nearly 
   impossible to fully delete.

3. **Use privacy settings to control who sees 
   your content**
   Protects your personal information from 
   strangers and potential scammers.

4. **Give credit when sharing someone else's 
   work or content**
   Respects intellectual property and builds 
   your reputation as an honest person.

5. **Report harassment, fake accounts, and 
   suspicious content immediately**
   Keeps the online community safe and 
   protects yourself from harm.

---

## ❌ DON'TS

1. **Never share your phone number, address, 
   or college ID publicly**
   Personal details can be misused for stalking, 
   scamming or identity theft.

2. **Never post offensive, racist, or 
   inappropriate content of any kind**
   Can lead to serious academic and legal 
   consequences including expulsion.

3. **Never share OTPs, passwords, or bank 
   details on any platform**
   This is the most common cause of UPI fraud 
   and financial loss among students.

4. **Never engage with or respond to 
   cyberbullies or online trolls**
   Engaging escalates the situation — 
   report and block instead.

5. **Never blindly trust or meet someone 
   you only know from social media**
   Online identities can be completely fake — 
   always verify before trusting.
